Butter Chicken Recipe

This indian dish is a must make for those wanting to add some spice to their life. This cream based dish tickles the tongue with its exotic flavors pair with aromatics guaranteed to elevate the mood of any who are in its prescience

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of chicken cut into bite sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup of plain yogurt
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons minced garlic
